What are operators?

What are operators?

Operators are used to perform operations on variables and values.
Basic Operators

1. Arithmetic Operators:

  • We can perform all the standard operations like addition, subtraction, and more.

print(5 + 7)
#This will print 12

print(6 - 10)
#This will print -4

print(4 * 3)
#This is for multiplication and thus prints 12

print(10 / 5)
#This is for division and thus prints 2


2. Comparison Operators:

Comparison operators are used to compare two values.

  • We can compare two values using '=='

x = 5
y = 3
print(x == y)
#This will print 'False' because 5 doesn't equal 3

  • We can use '!=' to say something is not equal to something else

x = 12
y = 7
print(x != y)
#This will print 'True' because 12 doesn't equal 7

  • We have '>' and '<' which are used to check if something is bigger or less than something else.

#First we will look at 'greater than'
x = 10
y = 2
print(x > y)
#This will print 'True' because 10 is larger than 2

#Now we will look at 'less than'
print(x < y)
#This will print 'False' because 10 is larger than 2



  • Lastly, we have '>=' and '<=' which are used to check if something is bigger, less, or equal to something else.

#First we will look at 'greater than or equal to'
x = 10
y = 2
z = 10
print(x >= y)
#This will print 'True' because 10 is larger than 2

print(x >= z)
#This will print 'True' because 10 is equal to 10

#Now we will look at 'less than or equal to'
print(x <= y)
#This will print 'False' because 10 is not less than, or equal to 2

print(x <= z)
#This will print 'True' because 10 is equal to 10


3. Logical Operators

  • Logical operators are used to combine conditional statements

#First we will look at 'and' where both statements must be correct to print 'True'
x = 7
print((x > 3) and (x < 12))
#This will print 'True' because 7 is larger than 3 and smaller than 12

#Now we will look at 'or' where only 1 statement must be correct to print 'True'
y = 3
print((y >= 3) or (y > 5))
#This will print 'True' because the first statement is satisfied in 3 equalling to 3
